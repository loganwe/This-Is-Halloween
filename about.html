About "This is Halloween"

What is this?

"This is Halloween" is a small trivia game inspired by spooky themes and the holiday. Players answer a short set of questions and advance through levels when they score perfectly.

How to play

  1. Click START on the home page to begin.
  2. Answer all questions on each level and submit your answers.
  3. Score 3/3 to advance to the next level. If you don't get a perfect score, try again!
